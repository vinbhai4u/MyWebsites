Technology provider

Oxifree Corrosion Protection Coating

The latest innovation in sprayable polymeric resin coating for the protection of metallic objects for both warehouse and transit range of applications of any size or shape. Oxifree , a polymeric resin encapsulation and flexible coating providing both active and passive protection against a wide range of corrosion problems, offering a shield to the elements as well as inhibiting corrosion on the metal surface while having no impact on the environment.

Polyurea Coatings

 

Polyurea Technology

PolyUrea is a truly remarkable coating, lining and joint sealant technology and can be used for a variety of applications . PolyUrea coatings and linings are commonly applied over concrete and steel for corrosion protection and abrasion resistance. They also have a numerous advantages over conventional materials due to their extremely fast setting times, high elongation and their durability abrasion characteristics.

MFLOW

 

Simulator of Multiphase flow in oil pipelines and flow lines

MFLOW is the latest simulation technology to solve problems caused by multiphase flow in pipelines and gas-lift wells. We offer "professionally guided excursions" along pipelines used to transport oil and gas in the petroleum and petrochemical industries. After these "excursions" our clients know what they need do to save time and money. Our software products and services help petroleum companies to provide safe and reliable operation of oil and gas production and transportation systems.
