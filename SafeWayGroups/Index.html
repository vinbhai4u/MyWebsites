Welcome

SAFEWAY has been one of the leading general trading companies and distributors catering to the industrial needs of ever growing oil & gas, petrochemical, renewable energy and hydrocarbon sectors within the United Arab Emirates for over 15 years.

SAFEWAY respects and shares the values that have helped to shape the UAE into a global player in the fields of oil & gas, petrochemicals and energy. SAFEWAY is fully owned and managed by our chairman Abudhulla Ali Hasan Ghareeb Al Hosani who has significant personal connections with fellow business leaders in the region as well as within the government.
